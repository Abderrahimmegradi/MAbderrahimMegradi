Reference Research & Concept Development

The project began with extensive research into 1980s and 1990s computer hardware, studying iconic models like the IBM PC, Commodore 64, and early Apple computers. I collected reference images of CRT monitors, mechanical keyboards, and bulky computer towers to ensure authentic recreation of the retro computing era.

Vintage Hardware Modeling

Each component of the retro computing setup was meticulously modeled with attention to period-accurate details. The CRT monitor with its curved screen, the mechanical keyboard with sculpted keycaps, and the bulky computer tower were all created with authentic proportions and materials from the era.

Fluid Simulations & Data Streams

The hacking sequence features complex fluid simulations representing digital data streams. Using Blender's MantaFlow, I created dynamic flows of data that appear to emerge from the computer screen and interact with the physical environment.

  • Data stream fluid simulations
  • Particle systems for digital artifacts
  • Glowing energy effects
  • Interactive light emissions
