Concept Development & Storyboarding

The project began with extensive market research and concept development. I created detailed storyboards mapping out each shot, camera angle, and transition to ensure a cohesive narrative flow that effectively communicates the product's energetic nature.

Target Audience

Young adults (18-35) interested in active lifestyle

Brand Message

Energy, vitality, and modern lifestyle

Visual Style

High-energy, vibrant, and dynamic

The Energy Soda can was meticulously modeled with attention to branding details and physical accuracy. I created custom shaders for the aluminum can, condensation effects, and liquid contents to achieve photorealistic results.
